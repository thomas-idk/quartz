*A note on provenance: every title above was checked by hand for public-domain status — old enough authors, old enough translations, no modern rights notices found. The newer additions all come from Standard Ebooks or the Public Domain Library, both of which bake an explicit CC0 public-domain dedication into the file itself, using long-established out-of-copyright translations (Garnett, Jowett, George Long, Marriott, Giles, Samuel Moore...). Two standing exceptions, kept anyway: the* Tractatus *bundles a still-copyrighted translation alongside the public-domain one, and* Quran Errors *is anonymous with no metadata to verify at all. Everything else came back clean.*

*Covers are pulled from Open Library where available (two titles — Quran Errors and the Schopenhauer Selected Works — have no cover on file, shown with a placeholder icon instead). Cover art may carry its own separate rights from the text itself; these are shown small, for identification, same as any library catalog or bookstore listing.*
