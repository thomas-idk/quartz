Regarding [[Principia Ethica - G.E. Moore]]

Moore relies on [[Hinge Propositions]] and Wittgenstein would not be so happy with his stance, as from what I have heard about him, he presupposes objective morality through certainty. I suggest reading On Certainty - Wittgenstein before reading this

At least this is what i suppose is the conflict, as this was written before Moore even met with Wittgenstein in person.
